Common Questions

What We Can Build For You

Everything you need to know about working with us — tap any question to learn more.

What does your web development service include? +

We design and build responsive, high-performance websites and web apps — from business sites and landing pages to custom portals with databases, dashboards, and payment integration. Every site is built to load fast, rank well, and scale as your business grows.

What's covered under digital marketing (ads & social media)? +

We plan and run paid ad campaigns across Google and Meta, manage social media content and posting calendars, and handle SEO and analytics — all aimed at getting your brand in front of the right audience and turning attention into leads.

What kind of software development do you handle? +

Beyond websites, we build custom software — internal tools, employee/HR dashboards, booking and inventory systems, mobile-friendly web apps, and API integrations tailored to how your business actually operates.

Can you help manage and market an event? +

Yes — from ticketing pages and registration systems to on-the-ground coordination and promotion, we support corporate events, launches, and public functions end-to-end, including online payment and attendee management.

How soon will I hear back after submitting my project? +

Our team reviews every brief within a few hours and you'll hear back with a tailored proposal within 24 hours — plus a free 30-minute strategy call to align on scope, with no obligation to proceed.

Do I need to know exactly what I want before reaching out? +

Not at all. Many clients come to us with just an idea or a problem to solve. Our team will ask the right questions during the free consultation to help shape your project scope and requirements together.

Can you handle multiple services together in one project? +

Absolutely. Many of our clients combine services — for example, a new website alongside a launch marketing campaign, or an event platform paired with custom software. We coordinate everything under one team.

Do you offer support after the project is delivered? +

Yes. Every project includes a post-launch support window, and we offer ongoing maintenance plans for updates, monitoring, campaign optimization, and new features as your needs grow.

What information should I include in my project brief? +

Share your goals, target audience, any timeline or budget expectations, and examples you like if you have them. The more context you give, the more accurate and useful our proposal will be.
